Commissions

If you want to commission art from me, a few guidelines to start with.
I can try to draw most anything, but my expertise is most certainly in the venues of the anthro and furry art, and largely (read: almost solely) female characters at that. I have never drawn a photorealistic portrait, for example, so you are better off finding that elsewhere.
Payment via PayPal (link below), half before I start and the other half upon completion.

Will draw:
  • Character art that's anywhere on the whole range between human and feral critter
  • Saucy stuff and nudes
  • ponies, Pokémon and other fandom stuff
Will not draw:
  • Porn/sex (can't draw it to save my life)
  • extreme fetish art
  • gore or overt violence

The Basic Deal: 80€
Pencil with as much detail as you give me and I am able to do. This is my definite comfort zone, and my best art all resides in pieces like this.
This sort of piece does tend to come with at least stylized background, though odds are random foliage or other background details just kind of happen unless you are against me doing it. Consider it a bonus for letting me pencil doodle your commission.

Added inks and color: +50€
Inking the lines and doing a color with your choice of either: color pencils, promarkers, or the combination of both. Pencils create the softest traditional look, markers the best color contrast, and the combination of the two probably the smoothest overall result.

Additional character: +50€
Additional character is always a bit more extra work in figuring out poses and how to arrange the picture.

All put together: 180€
My best example of a work that has a detailed background, two characters and all of it in color, both markers and color pencils used.
