About me and links to my stuff

Of some note is that none of my galleries are squeaky clean. While I do not draw outright porn, there will definitely be a fair bit of artistic nudity. Though at least on Deviantart and Furaffinity you won't see them if you do not have an account, so safe in that regard.

  • Deviantart My oldest art account. Contains over 900 pieces since my humble beginnings of posting art online in 2004.
  • Furaffinity In contrast, my FurAffinity account is not as old... or rather, I haven't actually had an active gallery there for that long. Last few years of my art, so probably the best place to 'get' what I draw these days.
  • Mastodon Artsocial number one. I don't do that much here, but I do post my new drawings there.
  • Tumblr Artsocial number two. Exists at least as much for me to ogle and repost other artists than post my own. Most likely to contain non-art related opinions as a side order.

About the artist
Since you are still here, I imagine you want to know about me and not just my art.
I'm an IT/trade student in my late 30's (in the middle of a career change), freelancing as an artist as a small side gig. I live in Southern Finland with my sister Seija, my wife Charlotte, and our dorky Tibetan Spaniel Ukko and derpy Ragdoll Aslan.
I have been drawing since my teens, and what started as scribbles on notebooks on boring school lectures slowly evolved into my longest-standing hobby. I started drawing with just pencil, tried all kinds of things in between (did solely digital art for a few years)... and now I draw purely traditional, mostly pencil art again.
